Wings of Fire: A Tribute to Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

A Life Dedicated to Science and Youth

About

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a major role in India's missile development programs and earned the title "Missile Man of India". He inspired millions of students with his humble nature, wisdom, and dedication to education.

What I Admire

I admire Dr. Kalam for his simplicity, intelligence, and love for students. Even after becoming the President of India, he stayed humble and focused on inspiring young people to dream big and work hard.

Famous Quote

"Dream, Dream, Dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
